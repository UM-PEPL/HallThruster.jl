HallThruster.Species — Type

HallThruster.EulerEquations — Method
EulerEquations

A ConservationLawSystem for the inviscid Navier-Stokes equations, better known as the Euler equations. Velocity and temperature are variable, so the values held in the ConservationLawSystem are set to zero and subsequently unused.

HallThruster.solve_potential! — Method
solve_potential!(; ϕ::Vector{Float64}, U::Matrix{Float64}, params::NamedTuple)

function to solve the potential equation derived from the generalized Ohm's law and employing charge conservation using quasineutrality. Second derivatives approximated with 2nd order central difference scheme, first derivatives with central difference. Centers of computational mesh equivalent with centers of fluid mesh, but omitting boundary ghost cells. If required, interpolation is used to infer properties at mesh boundaries. Potential is a function of magnetic field, anomalous and classical collision frequency, neutral and ion density as well as ion velocity, and electron density and temperature leading to electron pressure. Solved by inverting a tridiagonal matrix.
